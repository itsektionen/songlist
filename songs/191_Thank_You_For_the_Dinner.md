So I say
Thank you for the dinner,
the food you’re giving.
Thanks for all the drinks you’re bringing.
Who could live without it?
I ask in all honesty, what would life be?
without a schnapps, or punch, what are we?
So I say
Thank you
for the dinner
For giving it to me.
